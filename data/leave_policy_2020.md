# Apex Technologies — Leave Policy (Effective: January 2020)

## 1. Annual Leave
- All full-time employees receive **12 days** of Privilege Leave per calendar year.
- Maximum carry-forward: **6 days** to the following year.
- Leave encashment: not permitted during employment.

## 2. Sick Leave
- Employees receive **8 days** of Sick Leave per year.
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding **3 consecutive days**.

## 3. Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to **12 weeks** (84 days) of paid maternity leave.
- Applicable for up to **2 surviving children**.
- Pre-natal leave: up to 4 weeks before expected delivery.

## 4. Paternity Leave
- Male employees receive **5 working days** of paternity leave.
- Must be taken within 30 days of childbirth.

## 5. Work From Home Policy
- WFH permitted for **maximum 2 days per week** subject to manager approval.
- WFH not permitted during probation period (first 6 months).

## 6. Remote Work Allowance
- Monthly WFH stipend: **INR 500** for internet and ergonomic expenses.
