# Apex Technologies — Leave Policy (Effective: March 2022)

## 1. Annual Leave
- All full-time employees receive **15 days** of Privilege Leave per calendar year.
- Maximum carry-forward: **8 days** to the following year.
- Leave encashment: permitted up to **5 days** at year-end.

## 2. Sick Leave
- Employees receive **10 days** of Sick Leave per year.
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding **2 consecutive days**.

## 3. Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to **26 weeks** (182 days) of paid maternity leave,
  in compliance with the Maternity Benefit (Amendment) Act, 2017.
- Applicable for up to **2 surviving children**.
- Pre-natal leave: up to 8 weeks before expected delivery.

## 4. Paternity Leave
- Male employees receive **10 working days** of paternity leave.
- Must be taken within 60 days of childbirth.

## 5. Work From Home Policy
- Hybrid model: WFH permitted **3 days per week**.
- WFH permitted after **3 months** of employment.

## 6. Remote Work Allowance
- Monthly WFH stipend: **INR 1,000** for internet and ergonomic expenses.
