# Apex Technologies — Leave Policy (Effective: January 2024)

## 1. Annual Leave
- All full-time employees receive **18 days** of Privilege Leave per calendar year.
- Maximum carry-forward: **10 days** to the following year.
- Leave encashment: permitted up to **10 days** at financial year-end.
- Accrual rate: 1.5 days per completed month.

## 2. Sick Leave
- Employees receive **12 days** of combined Sick and Casual Leave per year.
- Medical certificate required for absences exceeding **2 consecutive days**.
- Unused SL/CL expires December 31st each year.

## 3. Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to **26 weeks** of fully paid maternity leave.
- Applicable for up to **2 surviving children**.
- Pre-natal leave: up to **8 weeks** before expected delivery date.

## 4. Paternity Leave
- Male employees receive **10 working days** of fully paid paternity leave.
- Must be taken within **90 days** of childbirth.

## 5. Work From Home Policy
- Structured 3-2 hybrid model: office Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday.
- Remote work: Monday and Friday, subject to SLA compliance.
- WFH permitted from **day one** of employment.

## 6. Remote Work Allowance
- Monthly WFH stipend: **INR 1,500** for internet and ergonomic setup.
- Automatically credited with monthly payroll.

## 7. Menstrual Wellness Leave (New — 2024)
- Female employees entitled to **2 days** per month of menstrual wellness leave.
- No medical certificate required.
